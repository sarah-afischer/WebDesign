Vegas Sunset

  • Fashion Show Theme: "Nature, Fashion, and Vegas."
  • Fashion Show Goal: to use sustainable materials and make the design itself tell the viewer something about both Las Vegas and nature itself.
  • My Design Goal: This design's purpose is to combine the concept of the city of lights and extravagent performers with the simplicity of the light in the fleeting desert sunset
  • Design Execution: for the sustainable material, a fabric store gave me chiffon that was dyed incorrectly (resulting in "rings" in the color). The color pallete is that of the sunset while the flowing, dynamic design imitates the extravagent outfits of las vegas performers
